K–12 Education

Donela Schools

We launched our first campus in partnership with Bombo Church in 2008 after discovering a lack of quality schools for our sponsored children to attend. If Uganda's children were going to rise up and out of the poverty they were born into, it was vital they receive an education that would set them up for success.

Together with the support of donors, we have since opened two more schools in the towns of Wobulenzi and Kakooge. When children receive quality education and life skills training rooted in Scripture, their lives are changed and their communities transformed.

Scripture-Based

Founded on Christian Values

Donela Orange Schools are founded on Christian values, providing children with the education they need to succeed and a foundation of faith from which they will grow. Students study core academic subjects, including English language arts, math, and science. Prayer is integrated into the daily curriculum, and students attend chapel weekly. High school students have additional opportunities to grow spiritually.

Academic Success

Custom Academic Plans

We offer each student a custom academic plan designed to meet their individual needs and set them up for success. Donela High School offers a unique curriculum that allows students to concurrently work toward certification in a trade while completing core subjects.

Zoo-Phonics

Early Literacy Through Zoo-phonics

Our teachers are trained in Zoo-phonics, a specialized multi-sensory language arts and reading program introduced by an Align Sponsor. Zoo-phonics equips students to read at an early age, enhancing their English language communication skills.

Growing & Expanding

Thriving Through Challenges

Thanks to the generosity of our donors, Donela Schools were able to retain their staff and continue providing for the needs of children in the community during the 2020-2021 school year. Many campus refurbishments were completed, helping the school to attract significant enrollment following the Covid-19 shutdown. Despite the challenges of the last few years, Donela Schools have thrived and continue to grow and expand as we build new classroom blocks for our many new students.

Scholarship Program

Equipping young men and women with the education and training they need to transform their communities. Align provides the opportunity for all our children to learn a job skill through university degrees, trade schools and other training programs — allowing them to make a living and care for themselves, their families and their communities.

Scholarships in Action

Millie's Story

Sponsored as a child through Align, Millie is now our Sponsorship Coordinator. Through generous donations she was granted a scholarship, graduated from college, and now uses her time and talents to care for the children in our Sponsorship Program.

Scholarships

Continuing Education

Provide for student tuition and supplies as they continue their education at a trade school or college. Having a job skill will not only provide a means to make a living but also provide for their families and communities.

Align currently operates a single training program with the hope of establishing more programs that will serve the community and promote self-sustainability.

Training Program

Seamstress Training Program

Hope4Women, Bombo Pentecostal Church, and Bay Cities Community Church in Lomita, CA had partnered together to launch a 9-month training program in Uganda. Now the program is overseen by Millie Ojera and is under the leadership of our wonderful Teacher Joan. Men and women who receive a scholarship participate in this program and learn professional sewing skills that will equip them for future jobs as seamstresses and tailors.
