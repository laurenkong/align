Our Story

Born From a Shared Vision

While visiting the impoverished community of Bombo, Uganda in 2006, God aligned the hearts of four people who refused to turn a blind eye to the tragedy surrounding them. Through a shared vision to offer hope and promote change, Align International was born.

Overwhelmed by orphans and widows, Uganda is a country where the majority of its citizens are trapped in a cycle of poverty with limited to no resources to help them break free. With 70% of its population under the age of 25 and standing as the world's third poorest country, the road ahead is long. It is also full of hope.

Today, Align continues to bring people together across the world with one common goal: to make a difference in the lives of thousands of desperate children through basic necessities, spiritual support, and education.

Our Location

Where We Serve

Our Uganda partners work in various communities. Bombo is located one hour north via the Gulu Highway from the capital, Kampala, in the central region of Uganda. Bombo PAG Church has planted churches in the nearby surrounding communities of Wobulenzi, Kakooge, Luweero, and Kavule.

Our Partners

Alex & Millie Ojera

Align Partners since 2006

Alex is a Bishop in the Pentecostal Assemblies of God serving mostly in Central Uganda. He is the lead Pastor at Bombo Church where he both teaches and mentors youth, raising up leaders to change the fabric of the local community.

Millie is the CEO of Life with Hope Ministries, where she oversees operations of the Child Sponsorship Program, Life Homes, Donela Schools, and Scholarship Programs. She also manages the Donela Schools and serves as the Worship Leader at Bombo Church.

Board Members since October 2015

Brian & Stacey Meadows

Brian: Feb 2021 · Stacey: Jan 2025

Brian is a senior managing consultant for Simpler Consulting, an IBM company. As Chairman of the Board, Brian has been a constant in caring for the people of Uganda. His continuous giving towards building a better life for children and families has only given new life to many in need.
