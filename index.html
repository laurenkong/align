How We Transform Lives

Food & Water

We provide nutritious food and clean water to children on a daily basis. Children who receive sufficient food and water are happier, healthier, do better in school, and more likely to thrive as adults.

Life Homes

Providing safe housing to children in need

We make sure each child in our Sponsorship Program lives in a safe, secure, and loving home that will support their physical and spiritual growth. Any child needing an alternative living situation is offered full-time boarding at a school campus, placement with a loving foster family, or long-term care at our Life Home Village.

Housing up to 40 children across four homes, the Life Home Village is a refuge where children are cared for physically, emotionally, and spiritually — each home with a dedicated caretaker.

Donela Schools

Quality education for a bright future

The mission of Donela Schools is to provide Godly, quality and affordable education that will equip this generation to transform our community and nation. Currently the Schools serve nearly 1,200 children from preschool through twelfth grade at three locations in central Uganda.

We launched our first campus in partnership with Bombo Church in 2008 after discovering a lack of quality schools for our sponsored children to attend.

How Sponsorship Works

We partner with local churches in impoverished areas throughout Uganda to identify at-risk children. Some live in a safe, caring environment but their basic needs — food, clean water, healthcare, and school tuition — are not met. Others come from broken or abusive homes and require shelter with a loving caregiver.
